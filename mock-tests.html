Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I select my branch and semester?
    On the homepage, use the "Branch" and "Semester" dropdowns to filter content.
  • Where can I find study notes?
    Notes are available in the "Study Resources" section on the homepage.
  • How do I attempt mock tests?
    Navigate to the "Mock Tests" page from the header menu.
  • My progress isn't updating. What should I do?
    Ensure you are logged in (if a login feature is implemented) and have completed quizzes. If issues persist, please contact us.
